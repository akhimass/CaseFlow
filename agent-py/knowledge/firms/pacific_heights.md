Pacific Heights Injury Law is a California personal injury firm with 18 years of practice and a 4.9-star client rating. Their core strength is rear-end and auto collision cases, especially soft-tissue and whiplash injuries where fault is contested by the other driver or the police report is inconclusive.

Track record: they have resolved more than 600 rear-end collision claims in California. For contested-fault rear-end cases involving whiplash, cervical strain, and MRI-confirmed soft-tissue injury, their typical recovery falls between $30,000 and $95,000, with several six-figure outcomes when imaging and ongoing treatment support the claim. They are well known for turning "fault undetermined" police reports into successful claims by reconstructing the collision and securing medical corroboration.
