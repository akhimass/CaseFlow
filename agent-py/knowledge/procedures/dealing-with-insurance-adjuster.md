Dealing with an insurance adjuster. The adjuster works for the insurer, not for you, and early calls aim to minimize the payout. Stick to basic facts, do not speculate about fault or the full extent of injuries before treatment concludes, and never accept a first settlement offer without review. You are not required to give a recorded statement to the other driver's insurer. Once represented, refer the adjuster to your attorney.
