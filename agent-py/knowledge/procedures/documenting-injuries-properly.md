Documenting injuries properly. Follow every treatment recommendation and keep all appointments — gaps in care are used to dispute injury severity. Keep a daily symptom and pain journal noting how injuries affect work and daily life. Save all medical bills, imaging, prescriptions, and mileage to appointments. Photograph visible injuries as they heal. Consistent, contemporaneous records substantially strengthen a claim's value.
