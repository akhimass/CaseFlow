Finding the right doctor after an accident. See a physician who documents injuries thoroughly and is comfortable testifying if needed. Specialists — orthopedists, neurologists, pain management — carry more evidentiary weight than urgent care alone for serious injuries. Follow referrals for imaging such as MRI when symptoms persist. Avoid long delays between the accident and first treatment, which insurers exploit to argue the injury was unrelated.
