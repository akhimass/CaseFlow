First 72 hours after an accident. Seek medical care immediately, even for minor symptoms, so injuries are documented. Call the police and obtain a report number. Photograph the scene, vehicle damage, and visible injuries. Exchange insurance and contact information but do not admit fault. Avoid posting about the incident on social media. Contact a personal-injury attorney before giving any recorded statement to an insurer.
