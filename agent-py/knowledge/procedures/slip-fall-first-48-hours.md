First 48 hours after a slip-and-fall or trip-and-fall. Photograph the hazard immediately — the wet floor, broken step, spill, or missing sign — before it is cleaned up or repaired, because that evidence disappears fast. Report the fall to the property owner or manager and ask for a written incident report, then get a copy or the report number. Note the names of any witnesses and staff present. Seek medical care promptly so injuries are documented, even if pain seems minor at first. Keep the shoes and clothing you were wearing, unwashed. Do not give a recorded statement to the property's insurer and do not sign anything before speaking with a personal-injury attorney.
