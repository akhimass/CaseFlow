California dog-bite claim, Los Angeles. The dog had a documented history of prior aggression and a previous animal-control complaint. High severity: deep puncture wounds to the arm and hand with nerve damage, multiple surgeries, and permanent loss of grip strength. California's strict-liability statute (Civ. Code §3342) makes the owner liable regardless of prior behavior, and the documented prior incidents foreclosed any defense — liability clear. Settlement range $120,000–$350,000, with permanent functional loss and scarring driving non-economic value.
