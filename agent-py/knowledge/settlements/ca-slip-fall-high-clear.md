California slip-and-fall at a restaurant, Los Angeles. A customer slipped on an unmarked freshly-mopped floor with no warning cone. High severity: displaced hip fracture requiring surgical fixation, a multi-day hospitalization, and months of physical therapy. Staff testimony and the absence of a wet-floor sign established the owner's notice of the hazard — liability clear. Settlement range $150,000–$400,000, with the surgery, mobility loss, and ongoing care driving value.
