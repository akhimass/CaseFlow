California places no cap on economic or non-economic damages in ordinary personal injury cases (Cal. Civ. Code §3333). Economic damages cover medical bills, lost wages, and property loss; non-economic damages cover pain and suffering. The MICRA cap limits non-economic damages in medical malpractice cases only ($390,000 for non-death cases in 2024, rising annually under AB 35; Civ. Code §3333.2). Punitive damages require clear and convincing evidence of malice, oppression, or fraud (§3294).
