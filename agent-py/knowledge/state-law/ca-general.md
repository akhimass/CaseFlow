California personal injury primer. Most claims arise from negligence: duty, breach, causation, and damages (Cal. Civ. Code §1714). Police reports are influential but not conclusive — fault may be listed as undetermined when there are no independent witnesses. ER records and imaging establish injury severity. Best practice is to document the scene, injuries, and insurance details within 72 hours and to avoid recorded statements to insurers before consulting an attorney.
