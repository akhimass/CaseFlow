California statute of limitations for personal injury. Auto accidents, slip-and-fall, motorcycle, premises, and dog-bite claims must be filed within 2 years of the date of injury (Cal. Code Civ. Proc. §335.1). Property damage: 3 years (§338). Claims against a government entity require an administrative claim within 6 months (Gov. Code §911.2). Medical malpractice: 1 year from discovery or 3 years from injury, whichever is first (§340.5). For minors, the clock is tolled until age 18.
