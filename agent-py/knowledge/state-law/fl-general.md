Florida personal injury primer. Florida is a no-fault auto-insurance state: each driver's PIP covers initial medical costs up to $10,000 regardless of fault (Fla. Stat. §627.736). To pursue the at-fault driver for pain and suffering, the injury must be permanent or meet the statutory threshold. Treatment within 14 days of a crash is required to access PIP benefits. The 2023 tort reforms shortened the filing window and tightened the negligence standard.
