Florida statute of limitations for negligence personal injury is 2 years from the date of injury, reduced from 4 years by HB 837 effective March 24, 2023 (Fla. Stat. §95.11(4)(a)). Injuries occurring before that date may retain the prior 4-year window. Wrongful death: 2 years. Medical malpractice: 2 years from discovery (§95.11(4)(b)). Confirm the injury date carefully, because the 2023 change is a common source of error.
