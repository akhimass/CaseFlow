citation: "Tex. Civ. Prac. & Rem. Code §74.301; §41.008"
---

Texas caps non-economic damages in medical malpractice at $250,000 against physicians, with separate caps for facilities (Tex. Civ. Prac. & Rem. Code §74.301). Ordinary personal injury economic and non-economic damages are not capped. Punitive (exemplary) damages are capped at the greater of $200,000 or two times economic damages plus up to $750,000 in non-economic damages (§41.008), and require clear and convincing evidence.
