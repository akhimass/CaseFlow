Texas personal injury primer. Negligence claims require duty, breach, causation, and damages. Texas is an at-fault auto-insurance state, so the at-fault driver's insurer pays. Because of the 51% bar on recovery, establishing the other party's majority fault is critical. Police reports, witness statements, and medical records are the core evidence. Preserve evidence and seek treatment promptly.
