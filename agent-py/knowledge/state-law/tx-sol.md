Texas statute of limitations for personal injury is 2 years from the date the cause of action accrues (Tex. Civ. Prac. & Rem. Code §16.003). This covers auto, premises, slip-and-fall, motorcycle, and dog-bite claims. Medical malpractice: 2 years (§74.251). Claims against governmental units require notice within 6 months (§101.101). Minors' claims are generally tolled until age 18.
