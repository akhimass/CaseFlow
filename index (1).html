How It Works

From inquiry to case packet in minutes.

A streamlined process that eliminates intake chaos and delivers firms exactly what they need.

1

Consumer starts intake

Voice, chat, or video—consumers connect however they prefer. The system welcomes them in their language and begins the guided intake process.

2

AI asks qualifying questions

Adaptive questioning assesses case type, severity, jurisdiction, liability signals, and urgency. Documents are uploaded or held up to the camera.

3

System scores the case

Case strength, jurisdiction fit, liability indicators, and urgency are scored in real time. Comparable settlements are retrieved for context.

4

Match to vetted firms

Each qualified case is routed to firms that actively want that case type, jurisdiction, and severity profile—no more spray-and-pray leads.

5

Firm receives ready-to-review packet

Transcripts, parsed documents, case summary, strength score, and comparable data—all organized and audit-ready for immediate review.
